Virtual trainer
for upper limb mechanical prosthetic

My participation was mainly focused on the design and printing of the PCB to integrate into the full prototipe and reduce the size of the electronics as much as possible. By using arduino IDE a serial communication module was stablished with Unity hub to control the movement of the 3D model with real movements such as the ones used while controlling a real mechanical transhumeral upper limb prosthesis.
